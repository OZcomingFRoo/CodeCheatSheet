How, what why

Guards are meant to prevent from user from casually nagivating in the SPA application. It is commonly used for:

  • Limiting access to a page for admin/users only
  • Prevent users from leaving an unsaved form

You can do all that and much more by implamenting one of the life cycle hooks during routing. The available hooks are:

  • CanActivate
  • CanActivateChild
  • CanDeactivate
  • Resolve
  • CanLoad
